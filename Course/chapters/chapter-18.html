üîÑ Claims Process Overview

Phase Duration Key Activities
1. Document Gathering 1-2 weeks Collect required documents
2. Claim Preparation 1-3 days Complete forms, organize package
3. Filing 1 day Submit to appropriate office
4. Processing 30-90 days County reviews claim
5. Approval & Disbursement 1-4 weeks Funds released

üìÅ Phase 1: Document Gathering

Documents from Client

  • Government-issued photo ID
  • Social Security card or number
  • Proof of former address (utility bill, etc.)
  • Signed authorization forms

Documents from Public Records

  • Deed showing former ownership
  • Foreclosure sale confirmation
  • Surplus funds verification
  • Any court orders

Documents from County

  • Claim forms
  • Surplus funds statement
  • Filing instructions

üìù Phase 2: Claim Preparation

Claim Package Checklist

  • Completed claim form
  • Copy of client's ID
  • Proof of ownership/identity
  • Authorization to act
  • Supporting documentation
  • Cover letter (if required)
  • Notarization (if required)

Method Pros Cons

‚è≥ Phase 4: Processing

What Happens During Processing

  1. County receives and logs claim
  2. Staff reviews for completeness
  3. Verification of claimant identity
  4. Check for competing claims
  5. Legal review (if required)
  6. Approval or request for more info

Follow-Up Schedule

  • Week 2: Confirm receipt
  • Week 4: Check status
  • Week 6: Follow up if no update
  • Week 8+: Weekly check-ins

‚úÖ Phase 5: Approval & Disbursement

Upon Approval

  1. Receive approval notification
  2. Confirm disbursement method
  3. Notify client immediately
  4. Track check/wire status
  5. Collect your fee upon client receipt

Disbursement Methods

  • Check mailed to client: Most common
  • Check mailed to you: If assignment allowed
  • Wire transfer: For large amounts
  • Court-ordered distribution: Complex cases
