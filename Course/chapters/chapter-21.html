üìÖ Training Schedule

Week 1: Foundation

  • Day 1-2: Company overview, role explanation, tool setup
  • Day 3-4: Core task training with SOPs and videos
  • Day 5: Practice exercises and assessment

Week 2: Supervised Practice

  • Assigned tasks with daily review
  • Daily check-in calls
  • Immediate feedback
  • Gradual independence

Week 3-4: Increasing Independence

  • More tasks, less supervision
  • Weekly review meetings
  • Address issues as they arise

‚úÖ Quality Control

Checking Work

  • Research tasks: Spot-check 10-20% of entries
  • Calling tasks: Listen to recorded calls
  • Admin tasks: Review completed documents

Feedback Framework (SBI)

  • Situation: When/where did this happen?
  • Behavior: What specifically did they do?
  • Impact: What was the result?
üí°

Example: "Yesterday when you were skip tracing (Situation), you used results with confidence scores below 70% (Behavior). This could lead to us contacting wrong people (Impact). Going forward, please only use 70%+ confidence results."
