ğŸ“‹ Step-by-Step Research Process

Step 1: Identify Target Counties

  • Start with 3-5 counties
  • Choose based on foreclosure volume
  • Consider accessibility of records

Step 2: Access Foreclosure Records

  • Find the county's online portal
  • Search for recent foreclosure sales
  • Note sale dates and prices

Step 3: Calculate Potential Surplus

  • Sale Price - Mortgage - Liens - Costs = Surplus
  • Focus on surplus amounts over $3,000
  • Document all calculations

Step 4: Verify Surplus Exists

  • Check if funds have been claimed
  • Confirm amount with county
  • Note any deadlines
