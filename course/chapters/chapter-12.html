The Trust Equation

Trust = (Credibility + Reliability + Intimacy) / Self-Interest
  • Credibility: Do you know what you're talking about?
  • Reliability: Will you do what you say?
  • Intimacy: Do you care about them?
  • Self-Interest: Are you just in it for the money?
