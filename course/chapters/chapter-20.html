Step 3: Screen Applications

üí°

Green flags:

  • Personalized response
  • Answered all questions
  • Clear communication
  • Relevant experience

Red flags:

  • Generic, copy-paste responses
  • Poor English
  • Didn't follow instructions

Step 4: Interview Top Candidates

Questions to ask:

  • "Tell me about your previous VA experience."
  • "How do you stay organized with multiple tasks?"
  • "What do you do when you're stuck on a task?"
  • "What's your internet speed and backup plan?"

Step 5: Test Before Hiring

Give a small, paid test project (1-3 hours) to evaluate:

  • Quality of work
  • Communication
  • Following instructions
  • Timeliness
