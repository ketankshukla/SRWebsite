## Pro Tips When Generating

1. **Generate 6-10 variations** - Pick the best one, AI results vary significantly
2. **Regenerate hands** - If hands look wrong, use inpainting to fix just that area
3. **Check for text** - Zoom in and make sure no garbled AI text appeared on documents/signs
4. **Test at banner size** - Make sure faces aren't cut off when cropped to your header dimensions
5. **Upscale the winner** - Use an AI upscaler for crisp website display
6. **Consider skin tones** - Ensure lighting looks natural on all skin tones in the image
