Welcome back. Today we're confronting our beliefs about the environmentâ€”and the illusions that prevent us from seeing clearly.

We live as if we're separate from nature. As if the environment is "out there" somewhere. This is one of the most dangerous illusions of our time.

Climate change is happening now. Biodiversity is collapsing. Pollution is everywhere. The systems that sustain life are under unprecedented stress.

So why don't we act like this is an emergency?

The separation illusion makes us forget we're part of nature. The technology illusion makes us believe innovation will solve everything without requiring change. The growth illusion makes us believe economies can grow forever on a finite planet. The distance illusion makes us believe consequences will fall on others.

So what does facing reality look like?

Recognize dependence. Everything you eat, drink, and breathe comes from natural systems.

Understand limits. There are real constraints on what Earth can provide.

See connection. You're not separate from nature. You are nature.

Act accordingly. This isn't just about recycling. It's about fundamentally rethinking how we live.

The environmental crisis is also a crisis of belief. Solving it requires new ways of seeing ourselves and our world.
